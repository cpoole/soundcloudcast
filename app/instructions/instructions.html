How to use Soundcloudcast

  1. Download chromecast extension
  2. Go to www.soundcloud.com
  3. Play a track
  4. Click the chromecast icon
  5. Control playback from Soundscloud's player
  6. Sit back and enjoy
  7. Lower your computer's volume(I would automatically mute the tab's sound, but there is no official support for this yet)

Thanks for being awesome and using Souncloudcast!

Extra Note: I made this because there wasn't any good chromecast support for Soundcloud on the web.
Sometimes I like to sit back and just listen to Soundcloud, so why not be able to cast it to my tv.
